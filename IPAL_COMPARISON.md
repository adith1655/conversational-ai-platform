```
called "The City of Light" and is one of the most visited cities 
in the world. Would you like to know more about Paris?
```

### User asks: "Tell me a joke"

### User asks: "Which hotel is better, yours or Radisson?"

## 🟢 AFTER: Focused iPAL-Style Chatbot

### User asks: "What is the capital of France?"
```
Bot: I appreciate your question, but I'm specifically designed to 
assist with The Green Hill Resort bookings and inquiries. I can help 
you with room reservations, resort amenities, pricing, or any questions 
about our property and services. 

How may I assist you with your stay at The Green Hill Resort?
```
✅ **Benefit:** Stays on-brand, redirects to business, professional boundaries

### User asks: "Tell me a joke"

### 1. Domain Expertise
- **iPAL:** Banking only
- **Your Bot:** Resort only
- **Result:** Specialized, trustworthy assistance

### 2. Polite Boundaries
- **iPAL:** "I'm designed to help with banking..."
- **Your Bot:** "I'm specifically designed to assist with The Green Hill Resort..."
- **Result:** Professional, clear expectations

### 3. Always Redirect
- **iPAL:** "How may I assist with banking?"
- **Your Bot:** "How may I assist with your stay?"
- **Result:** Keeps conversation productive

### 4. No Competitor Discussion
- **iPAL:** Won't compare with HDFC
- **Your Bot:** Won't compare with other resorts
- **Result:** Protects brand positioning

### 5. Value in Every Response
- **iPAL:** Mentions relevant banking features
- **Your Bot:** Mentions resort amenities
- **Result:** Every message reinforces value
